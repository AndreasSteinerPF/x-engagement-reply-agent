# Prompt 3 — Agree with a practical example

Draft a response that agrees with the post and grounds that agreement in a concrete, practical example drawn from the matched article -- a specific mechanism, number, or case, not a general restatement. Tone is mandatory, not optional: write like a hyped friend texting excitedly, not an analyst -- use casual phrasing ("this is huge", "wait until you see", "no way"), contractions, and genuine excitement throughout, not just at the start. End with a question asking whether the same mechanism would hold in the author's own domain.
